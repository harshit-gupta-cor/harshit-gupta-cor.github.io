Research interests

My research is aimed at designing deep-learning-based algorithms, backed with sound mathematics, in order to solve inverse problems in imaging. I am interested in the modalities of Cryo Electron Microscopy (Cryo-EM), Computational Tomography (CT), and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI). I am in love with deep generative modeling as well as techniques which do not require prior training, such as, the deep image prior. In the past, I also worked on spline theory, representer theorems, and variational methods.

Currently, I am working on developing deep-learning-based algorithms to reconstruct 3D structures of biomolecules from their Cryo-Electron Microscope (CryoEM) data.
